A Ring for Jeanne

When Dean Martin married Jeanne Biegger in 1949, he gave her more than a promise. He gave her a ring that would witness nearly a quarter century of marriage — a Hollywood partnership, a period that spanned the rise of his career, and the Rat Pack era.

This ring was there — at the wedding, through the years, and until Jeanne's passing in 2016. Now, its story continues.
